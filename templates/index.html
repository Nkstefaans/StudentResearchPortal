How It Works

Submit Research

Students submit their thesis or dissertation for review by their supervisors and external examiners.

Nominate Examiners

Supervisors nominate external examiners who have the expertise to review the research.

Approve Nominations

The postgraduate office reviews and approves examiner nominations based on qualifications.

Review & Feedback

External examiners provide detailed feedback within a six-week timeframe.

Key Features

Multiple User Roles

Support for students, lecturers, examiners, and administrative staff.

Process Workflow

Structured workflow from submission to final review and approval.

Deadline Tracking

Automatic reminders for approaching review deadlines.

Notifications

Real-time updates and email notifications for all stakeholders.
